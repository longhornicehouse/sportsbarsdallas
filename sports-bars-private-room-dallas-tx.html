Frequently Asked Questions

What sports bars in Dallas TX offer private rooms?
Sports bars such as Bottle Service, The Londoner Addison, and Bar Louie Uptown all offer private rooms for events.
How much does it cost to rent a private room at a sports bar in Dallas TX?
Costs vary depending on the bar and size of the room. Generally, prices range from $100-$500 per hour.
Are there any additional amenities available with a private room rental at a sports bar in Dallas TX?
Amenities may include TVs with satellite channels, catering services, full-service bars, karaoke machines, and more.
Does my group need to meet a minimum number of people to rent out a private room at a sports bar in Dallas TX?
Most sports bars require groups to have at least 10 people in order to rent out their private rooms.
