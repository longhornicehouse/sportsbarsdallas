sports bars with patios Irving TX

Sports bars with patios are a great way to enjoy the Dallas area! From Irving, Texas to all over the city, there are plenty of places that offer outdoor seating and delicious food and drinks. Whether you're looking for a place to watch the big game or just want to relax on some patio furniture, these spots have got you covered!

The first spot is Aces Sports Bar & Grill. With two huge patios and tables spread out through its interior, it's an excellent choice for any occasion. This spot offers classic bar fare like burgers, wings, and sandwiches alongside craft beers. Plus, they have TVs throughout the restaurant so you can catch any sporting event that might be playing!

Next up is The Fillmore Pub. Known far and wide for its unique atmosphere and friendly service, The Fillmore Pub has something for everyone. The menu features pub classics such as tacos, pizza slices, and mac 'n' cheese bites - plus tons of apps to choose from too! And don't forget their extensive beer selection; they've got everything from lagers to stouts. Plus their patio is perfect for watching the sunset on a warm summer night!

Lastly we come to Rodeo Goat Ice House. Here you'll find an array of tasty dishes such as fried pickles & jalapenos, smoked beef brisket nachos, and even gourmet burgers! To top off your evening head outside where they have several seating options including picnic tables and Adirondack chairs. And if sports aren't your thing then no worries - this spot also boasts live music performances every Friday night!

In conclusion: Whether you're in Irving or anywhere else in Dallas - sports bars with patios are definitely worth checking out! Relaxing outdoors while indulging in some amazing food & drinks? Sounds like a great time indeed!!
