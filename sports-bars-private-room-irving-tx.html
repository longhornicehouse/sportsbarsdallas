sports bars private room Irving TX

Sports bars are a great option for any group wanting to watch the big game! But if you're looking for a more private experience, Irving TX has some great sports bars with private rooms. Whether it's an office party or birthday celebration, these venues offer all the amenities you need and a cozy atmosphere to boot!

The first on our list is 'Boomers Sports Bar & Grill'. They boast five separate rooms that can accommodate up to twenty people each. The rooms come equipped with flat screen TVs and karaoke machines as well as sound systems so everyone can partake in the fun. Plus, there's plenty of food options too - from burgers and wings to quesadillas and tacos.

Next up is 'Jumpers Sports Bar & Grill'. Their private room offers seating for up to forty guests, along with its own bar area and full-service kitchen. There's also a pool table and darts board for those who want to stay active while watching their favorite teams battle it out. Furthermore, they provide catering services as well so you don't have to worry about ordering food in advance.

Last but not least is 'Hitters Sports Bar & Grill'. Here they have two special event rooms available that hold up to thirty-five people each. Both are decorated in classic sports style with memorabilia adorning the walls. Additionally, they provide an extensive beer selection as well as delicious pub fare like nachos, sliders, wings and more!

All three of these venues offer an ideal spot for your next gathering - whether it be a corporate event or simply just friends getting together to watch the game! So if you're looking for privacy combined with sports bar vibes in Irving TX then look no further than these awesome spots!
