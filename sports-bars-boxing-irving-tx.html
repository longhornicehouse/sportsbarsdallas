Frequently Asked Questions

What sports bars in Irving TX offer boxing?
Firewheel Sports Grill, The Ticket Sports Bar & Grill, and Twin Peaks are all sports bars in Irving TX that offer boxing.
How much does it cost to watch a boxing match at the sports bar in Irving TX?
Pricing varies depending on the particular venue and event; however, tickets typically range from $10 to $30 per person.
Are there any special events or promotions for watching a boxing match at a sports bar in Irving TX?
Yes, Firewheel Sports Grill has monthly fight nights with specials such as two-for-one drinks and free giveaways during the matches. The Ticket Sports Bar & Grill often hosts special viewing parties for major boxing events, while Twin Peaks often has food and drink specials during big fights.
