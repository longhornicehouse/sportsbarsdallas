sports bars gluten free Dallas TX

Sports bars are a great option for people looking to enjoy their favorite games without sacrificing their dietary needs! But finding a sports bar that serves gluten free options in Dallas, TX can be difficult. Thankfully, there are some great restaurants available that offer delicious alternatives.

One such spot is the Whistle Britches restaurant. Here you can find a variety of tasty dishes such as crispy fried cauliflower and spicy black bean tacos that don’t contain any wheat or gluten! Plus, they have an array of TVs for watching all the action from your favorite teams.
Furthermore, The Rustic is another great place to watch football while enjoying gluten free treats. They serve up crunchy chicken tenders and sliders with buns made from corn flour instead of wheat – yum! And on top of that, they also have plenty of craft beers on tap and live music to keep you entertained during halftime.

Additionally, if you're looking for something more upscale, then look no further than Abacus Restaurant & Bar. This modern eatery has a menu full of delectable delights like pan-seared scallops and truffle mac 'n' cheese – all made with gluten free ingredients! And if you want to catch the big game while dining here? No problem – they've got several flatscreen TVs located throughout the restaurant so you won't miss a beat!

So next time you’re in Dallas and feeling hungry but want to stick to your dietary restrictions, head over to one of these fantastic sports bars for some truly scrumptious fare!
