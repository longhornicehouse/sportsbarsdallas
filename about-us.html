About Us

Longhorn Icehouse is an upscale Texas-themed 25,000 square foot roadhouse bar, restaurant, event, entertainment and party venue that is open 7 days a week featuring an incredible kitchen, draft beers, craft cocktails and friendly staff. There’s 50 dining tables, a dedicated arcade space, a private back room, 50 wall-to-wall HD TVs, a 3x BIGGER 42-foot 4K HD Video Wall, 2 full-service bars with seating and a sprawling patio outside with TVs and games.

Longhorn Icehouse is the ideal place to watch NFL, NBA, MLB & NHL sports games, college football, soccer, UFC and boxing matches or to get together with friends and coworkers for happy hour specials. Enjoy plenty of space and Texas-themed décor inside and be sure to visit the outdoor patio which is studded with HD TVs and seating for outside gatherings. Feast on our incredible lunch specials during the day, wind down at happy hour then enjoy Longhorn Icehouse’s nightlife with DJs, special events, watch parties and celebrations with our welcoming Longhorn serving team and wonderful regular customers.

Our private back room is a convertible space that can seat up to 75 guests and is available to rent for receptions, showers, birthdays, meetings, corporate events, seminars, casino games, comedy shows, fundraisers and more. The private room also has an overhead projector and screen for meetups, luncheons, presentations and as a backup option to your current conference room.

We offer you the perfect destination to watch your favorite sports and host all types of celebrations while enjoying great food and drinks served up with Texas southern hospitality. Experience why “BIGGER is BETTER!”
