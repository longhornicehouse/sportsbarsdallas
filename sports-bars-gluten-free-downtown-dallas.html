sports bars gluten free Downtown Dallas

Sports bars in Downtown Dallas offer a wide variety of dining options for those looking to enjoy one of their favorite sports games. From tasty apps and snacks to delicious entrées, there is something for everyone. But what about those who are on a gluten-free diet? Worry not! There are plenty of places that provide gluten-free options so you can still join your friends in cheering on your team!

The first place to check out is The Rustic. They offer hearty entrees like the bison short rib with mashed potatoes and vegan tacos with roasted cauliflower. Plus, they have a gluten-free menu which includes everything from deviled eggs to pretzel bites. And don't forget their wide selection of craft beers and ciders!

Another great spot is High Fives Bar & Grill. They have an expansive menu filled with tasty dishes like pulled pork sliders and fried pickles, as well as some amazing sides like sweet potato fries and mac 'n' cheese balls. All of their food items can be ordered gluten-free, so you can join your pals for an enjoyable night at a local sports bar without sacrificing flavor or texture!

But if you're looking for something more upscale, then look no further than The Mansion Bar & Grill. This restaurant has it all: award-winning cocktails, exquisite small plates, fantastic entrees, and yes - even gluten-free options! They also feature live music throughout the week which will accompany your meal perfectly.

So whether you're looking for classic pub grub or something more refined, downtown Dallas has plenty of sports bars that are sure to satisfy any craving – even those on a gluten-free diet! No need to miss out on all the excitement! Let's go eat!!
